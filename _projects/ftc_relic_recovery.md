FIRST Tech Challenge Relic Recovery season (2017-2018) as Captain of a 10-member team. Built robots to collect Glyphs (foam cubes) and build patterns in the Cryptobox scoring zone. Key achievement: 6 Glyph Autonomous at World Championships. Built with Java, OpenCV, Fusion360, 3D printing, mill, lathe, and water jet.
