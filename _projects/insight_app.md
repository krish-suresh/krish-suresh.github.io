A platform designed to facilitate meaningful one-on-one discussions between individuals with differing perspectives (2021). The application prompts users to answer open-ended questions in a respectful manner during time-limited conversations. The matching system connects participants based on their profiles, pairing people from different backgrounds. Developed for the 2021 Google Solutions Challenge using Flutter and Firebase.
